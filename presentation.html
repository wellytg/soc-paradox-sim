Cyber Studies Research · University of Tulsa
The Research Gap

Mismatched Objectives

In many SOCs, analysts are measured by tickets closed (Speed), while the CISO cares about breach detection (Quality). This creates a "Productivity Trap".

  • Protocol A: Rapid Triage: High-volume, blurred streams. Can you find the threat while moving fast?
  • Protocol B: Deep Review: Low-volume, clear streams. Does more time always equal better detection?
